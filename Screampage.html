Description

The original Scream (1996) film is a slasher movie where a masked killer called Ghostface terrorizes high school students in the town of Woodsboro, California. The killer uses a deadly game of horror movie trivia to torment victims over the phone before attacking them. The main target is high school student Sidney Prescott, whose mother was murdered a year prior. The film's plot culminates with the revelation that the killers are Sidney's boyfriend, Billy Loomis, and his friend, Stu Macher, who are seeking revenge.
